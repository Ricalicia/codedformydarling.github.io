Intro

How You Make Me Feel

When I’m with you I don’t feel so stressed. I feel happier. I’m not as depressed anymore. I feel safe and chosen.

You’re very consistent with everything that you do, especially when it comes to me. Like when you were pursuing me — I mean seven years is a lot — and for you to just always be honest about your feelings, it’s really nice.

Your Character and Personality

One thing that I really love about your character is that you’re kind in a way that’s very cringe and corny, but I love it. You’re genuine about your feelings for me.

Long Distance Effort

You make that very clear with us being in a long-distance relationship. Even when you’re busy, you make time for me. You always text me first thing in the morning. If we don’t talk all day, you’re gonna make time for us to talk even if it’s late at night. You always call me. You always text me.

What Makes You Different

What makes you different from other people and other men is the fact that you don’t just give generic compliments or generic answers. When I ask you something specific, you actually go into detail about how you love me, why you love me, and what you love about me. You answer all of my silly questions. Respecting Boundaries

I also love the fact that when we talked and I said I felt like things were moving a bit too fast, you actually slowed down, listened to me, and made sure I was comfortable with everything you were doing.

Our History Together

We’ve been friends for a very long time — since seventh grade. I’m now a sophomore in college, and our friendship stayed close until Christmas Eve last year when we officially started dating. That history makes our relationship feel really special.

Your Growth and Change

The main thing for me is that you’re willing to change for the better. You had some bad habits before, but now you actually care about other people’s feelings. You’re not manipulative anymore. You actually try to be a better person, and you show that you don’t just talk about it — you actually do it.

Our Dynamic and Playfulness

I love that we crack jokes on each other, that we’re lovable with each other. Even if it’s corny, we fake-argue sometimes, but then we go back to being lovey-dovey.

Nicknames and Affection

I also love the little things that are just ours, like the crazy nicknames you give me. I love that you’re kind and that you make me feel special. Even as a little tiny boy that you are, you make me feel great. I also love all of your imperfections that you used to be insecure about. I don’t know, I just really like it. I understand that you have skin issues, and I’m okay with that. It’s okay — we all have blemishes and little things we’re insecure about. I don’t want to make you feel more self-conscious about being with me.

I stay in my room and enjoy our time, being there for myself, being there for you, being a support for others, and knowing that you support me too. When I have issues, I can go to you, even if it’s awkward, and you’re understanding. You wait until I’m actually ready to talk about everything entirely.

Noticing the Little Things

I just want you to know that I notice the little things that you do — the small shifts in energy, the little things you do to make us better. I like that you’re still you. You’re human, you still make mistakes, but you learn from them instead of using them as an excuse to act negatively.

Closing Message

And most importantly, I’m just really grateful for you, for us, and for everything we’re building together.
